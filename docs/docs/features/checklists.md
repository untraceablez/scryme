# Custom checklists

The **Checklists** page (`/checklists`, or the *checklists* link on the home page) tracks your
completion of any card list you define — the Power 9, the original dual lands, a Commander's
staples, a cube — generalizing [set completion](sets.md) to an arbitrary list.

## Create one

Give the checklist a name and paste the cards, **one per line**:

```
Black Lotus
Mox Sapphire
Ancestral Recall
```

A leading quantity (`4 Lightning Bolt`) and trailing printing hints (`(LEA) 1`, `*F*`) are accepted
too, so a decklist pastes straight in; duplicates are collapsed. Each line is matched to a card by
its **oracle identity**, so any printing you own counts. Lines that don't match a known card are
kept and flagged as *unrecognized*.

## Track completion

The checklist page shows the **percent complete**, an owned / total count, and every entry with a
✓ (owned) or ○ (missing), each linking to its card page. **Add missing to wishlist** drops the
matched-but-missing cards onto your [wishlist](wishlist.md) in one click.

Creating and editing checklists is disabled on the read-only demo.
